### Recovery Protocol

**Recovery Triggers (Auto-Deload):**
- HRV ↓ > 20% → Skip training, full rest
- RHR ↑ ≥ 5 bpm → Reduce intensity or rest
- Feel ≥ 4 →  Deload or rest day
- Two+ triggers → Full rest day

**Feel Scale:**
| Score | Meaning |
|-------|---------|
| 1 | Excellent (fully recovered) |
| 2 | Good (normal fatigue) |
| 3 | Moderate (manageable tiredness) |
| 4 | Fatigued (reduced readiness, deload trigger) |
| 5 | Exhausted (complete rest required) |

## 5. Nutrition / Fueling

### Training Fuel Recipe

```
GU-style gels (~32g carbs each)
- Guarana gel: Caffeine (guarana) + 32g carbs — 45 min before start (caffeine peaks for race)
- Mineral gel: Potassium + Calcium + Magnesium + 32g carbs — 10-15 min before or during rides >75min (cramp prevention)
- Regular gel: Carbs only, no additives — backup or during long rides
```

**CHO per bottle:** ~32g per gel  
**Target absorption:** ~60-90g CHO/hour during longer sessions

### Recovery Drink Recipe

_No dedicated recovery supplement. Post-training: regular food + water._

### Fueling by Workout Type

| Workout Type | Duration | CHO Target | Setup |
|--------------|----------|------------|-------|
| Recovery / Z1–Z2 | < 1.5 h | 30-40g/h (optional) | Water or electrolyte mix, gel if needed |
| Endurance | 1.5–3 h | 60-80g/h | ~2 gels/h + water/electrolyte |
| Long Endurance | 3–6 h | 60-90g/h | Rotate between gel types, mineral gels for cramp prevention |
| Threshold / SS | 1–2 h | 40-60g/h | 1-2 gels, regular or guarana depending on session intensity |
| VO₂ / High Intensity | 1–1.5 h | 30-50g/h | 1 gel before, easy to digest |
| Race / Event | 4–6 h | 60-90g/h | Full rotation: guarana pre-race, mineral gels during, regular as backup |

### Hydration

**Target:** 500-750 ml/hour  
**Sodium:** Electrolyte mix via mineral gels; additional salt intake on hot/long rides varies
